## Prompt

How do I start a trial, and what happens when it expires?

## Golden Response

You can start a 60-day trial for up to 5,000 users. Details on initiating the trial are in the [Set up Chrome Enterprise Premium guide](https://support.google.com/chrome/a/answer/14804659). Upon expiration, you are not automatically charged. Premium features (custom DLP, CAA) immediately stop functioning, but your configuration settings are saved, as mentioned (under Trials of CEP).
