## Prompt

How can I prevent users from copying data from company apps to personal apps like ChatGPT?

## Golden Response

You can configure Data Loss Prevention (DLP) rules in the Admin Console to control copy and paste actions. Specify your company apps as the 'Source URL' and sites like ChatGPT as the destination URL or URL Category. Details on configuring these rules are in [Use Chrome Enterprise Premium to integrate DLP with Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/a/answer/10104358).
